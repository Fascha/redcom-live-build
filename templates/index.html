What is this about?

This Tool is about analyzing and exploring communication on Reddit. There are two different layers of exploring the data. First you can get an overview with ranked comments and second you can go deeper into the discussion tree by clicking on one of the comments. This second layer represents the tree structure of Threads and gives an overview of the selected comment!

What is Reddit?

Reddit is a social news aggregation, web content rating, and discussion website. Reddit's registered community members can submit content, such as text posts or direct links. Registered users can then vote submissions up or down to organize the posts and determine their position on the site's pages. The submissions with the most positive votes appear on the front page or the top of a category. Content entries are organized by areas of interest called "subreddits".
